# Scam Prevention

## Common Scam Signals
- New account asks members to connect wallets or send seed phrases.
- Fake team profiles DM members with urgent requests.
- Links use typo domains or shorteners to hide target URLs.

## Prevention Checklist
- Enable high verification level in server settings.
- Pin a security notice in welcome and announcement channels.
- Restrict posting links for new members with role gating.
- Use AutoMod keyword filters for phishing terms and suspicious URLs.

## Moderator Response Steps
1. Delete malicious messages immediately.
2. Ban or timeout the scam account.
3. Post a short warning to the community with safe instructions.
4. Log incident details in a private mod channel.

## User Education Copy
"Team will never ask for seed phrase, private keys, or wallet transfer in DM. Report suspicious messages to moderators."
