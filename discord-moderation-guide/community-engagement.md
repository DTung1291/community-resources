# Community Engagement

## Weekly Engagement Mix
- One educational post (product, security, roadmap).
- One interactive post (poll, quiz, discussion).
- One recognition post (top contributors or helpers).

## Engagement Tactics
- Ask open-ended prompts instead of yes/no questions.
- Reward quality participation with roles or shoutouts.
- Rotate time slots to include multiple regions.

## Metrics to Track
- Active participants per week.
- Message volume in core channels.
- Event participation rate.
- Retention of new members after 7 days.

## Moderator Tone Guide
- Be clear and neutral during conflict.
- Be warm and encouraging during normal discussion.
- Close loops quickly when questions are answered.
