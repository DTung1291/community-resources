# Raid Handling

## Early Warning Signs
- Sudden spike of joins from low-age accounts.
- Repeated spam text across multiple channels.
- Mass ping attempts and disruptive media spam.

## Immediate Containment
1. Enable slow mode on active channels.
2. Lock vulnerable channels to trusted roles.
3. Pause invite links and create temporary approval flow.
4. Activate emergency AutoMod rules for spam and mentions.

## Incident Command Roles
- Lead Mod: decisions and announcements.
- Action Mod: ban, timeout, cleanup.
- Recorder: timeline and evidence capture.

## Post-Raid Recovery
- Remove temporary restrictions gradually.
- Publish recap with actions taken.
- Review gaps and update prevention rules.
