# Team Communication Tips

## Core Principles
- Prefer clarity over speed.
- Write decisions where everyone can find them.
- Use one source of truth for tasks.

## Practical Habits
- Add context, owner, and due date in each request.
- Confirm assumptions early.
- Share risks as soon as they appear.

## Async Communication Rules
- Use clear subject lines.
- Keep one topic per message.
- End with explicit next action.

## Meeting Hygiene
- Publish agenda before meeting.
- Timebox discussion points.
- Close with decisions and owners.
