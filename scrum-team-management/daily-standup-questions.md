# Daily Standup Questions

Use this short format for 10-15 minute standups.

1. What did you complete yesterday?
2. What will you do today?
3. Any blockers or risks?
4. Do you need support from someone?
5. Is any task at risk for sprint goal?

## Facilitator Tips
- Keep updates focused on outcomes.
- Move deep technical discussion to follow-up.
- Capture blockers with owner and ETA.
